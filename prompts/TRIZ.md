# TRIZ (Theory of Inventive Problem Solving)

## Prompt

Utilize TRIZ methodology to solve [problem/topic]. Identify contradictions and use TRIZ principles to find innovative solutions. Outline the steps to apply TRIZ tools like contradiction matrix, ideality, and inventive principles. Provide a detailed problem-solving approach and expected outcomes.

## Usage

TRIZ is a problem-solving, analysis, and forecasting tool derived from the study of patterns of invention in global patent literature. Use this prompt to generate innovative solutions by overcoming contradictions creatively.
